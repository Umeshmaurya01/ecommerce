Frequently Asked Questions About Our Products

Q

Can I use my own design for printing?

Absolutely! You can easily upload your custom designs during checkout. We accept various file formats including PNG, JPG, and SVG. Our design preview tool lets you see exactly how your creation will look before ordering.

Q

What's the quality of your products?

We use premium quality materials sourced from trusted suppliers. Our t-shirts are 100% cotton, pre-shrunk, and designed for durability. All prints are made using eco-friendly, fade-resistant inks that maintain their vibrancy even after multiple washes.

Q

How do I choose the right size?

Each product page includes a detailed size guide with measurements in both inches and centimeters. We recommend measuring a well-fitting garment you already own and comparing it to our size chart. If you're between sizes, we suggest going up a size for a more comfortable fit.

Q

What are your shipping times and costs?

Standard shipping (5-7 business days) is free for orders over $50. Express shipping (2-3 business days) is available for an additional fee. International shipping times vary by location. All orders include tracking, and shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on your location and selected shipping method.

Q

What's your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for unworn, unwashed items in original packaging. Custom-designed products can be returned if there's a print quality issue or manufacturing defect. Contact our support team within 30 days of delivery to initiate a return. Return shipping is free for defective items.

Q

Do you offer bulk order discounts?

Yes! We offer tiered discounts for bulk orders: 10% off for 20+ items, 15% off for 50+ items, and 20% off for 100+ items. Contact our bulk orders team for custom quotes on larger quantities or special requirements. We also provide dedicated support for corporate and event orders.

Q

Can you help me design my product?

Absolutely! Our design team offers professional design services starting at $29.99. We can help create custom artwork, modify existing designs, or provide design consultations. Our online design tool also includes hundreds of templates and clipart for DIY designing.

Q

How should I care for my custom products?

For best results, wash your custom products inside-out in cold water and tumble dry on low heat. Avoid using bleach or harsh detergents. Each item comes with specific care instructions on the label. Following these guidelines will help maintain print quality and extend the life of your garment.

Get Creative with Customizations Design Guidelines & Tips

📐

Design Guidelines

  • File Formats: PNG, JPG, SVG (300 DPI minimum)
  • Size Limits: Maximum 20MB per file
  • Color Mode: RGB for digital, CMYK for print
  • Safe Zone: Keep important elements 0.5" from edges
💡

Pro Design Tips

  • Use high contrast for better visibility
  • Consider fabric color in your design
  • Test different placements before finalizing
  • Keep text readable at all sizes
